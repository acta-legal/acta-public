# Acta Legal

Acta is a legal research assistant for Romanian attorneys. We build a searchable corpus of Romanian legislation and case law from public-sector sources, with AI-assisted retrieval and drafting.

This repository contains our **public documentation**:

- [`INGESTION_POLICY.md`](./INGESTION_POLICY.md) — what we fetch from public-sector portals, at what rate, and under what legal basis.
- [`INGESTION_STATUS.md`](./INGESTION_STATUS.md) — current corpus state, auto-refreshed daily.
- [`sources.yml`](./sources.yml) — machine-readable list of upstream data sources.

The Acta product itself is closed-source; this repository publishes only our data-access documentation.
